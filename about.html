WHO ARE WE?
Pop Paradise is a company founded in 2021 with the heart of a collector in mind. We distribute highly collectible products created by Funko with characters from ALL types of media! Whether you're interested in collecting characters from anime, movies, television and even video games, we have it all.

Everyone has a passion, and ours is to bring yours to life through creatively designed figures. Have some extra shelf space? Why not add some figures from your favorite Star Wars movie? Maybe you're super excited for the upcoming Spider-Man movie... So are we! Maybe it's time to get some Spidey figures on your shelves!

No matter your passion or hobby, we have something here for you!
